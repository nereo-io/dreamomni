I Ching AI - Frequently Asked Questions

What is I Ching?

The I Ching, or Book of Changes, is an ancient Chinese divination system using 64 hexagrams to provide insight and wisdom for life decisions.

What is a hexagram?

A hexagram is a symbol of six stacked lines, each either broken (yin) or solid (yang), representing a dynamic state of change and energy.

Can AI really interpret I Ching?

Yes. I Ching AI uses natural language models trained on classic I Ching texts, hexagram symbology, and modern decision analysis.

What are the most common hexagrams?

  • Hexagram 1: The Creative – Initiative and drive
  • Hexagram 2: The Receptive – Flexibility and support
  • Hexagram 23: Splitting Apart – Letting go of the old
