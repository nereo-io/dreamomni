# Refund Policy

## Introduction

At **DreamOmni**, we want AI video generation purchases to be clear and fair. This refund policy explains how subscription and credit refunds are handled.

DreamOmni.ai is an independent product and is not affiliated with Google, Gemini, or Google DeepMind.

## Subscription Refunds

### Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a subscription refund, you must:

- Request the refund within **3 days** of your initial purchase
- Have used **20 credits or fewer** during the subscription period
- Submit your refund request through our official support channel

### Processing Fees

An **8%** processing fee may be deducted from the refund amount to cover transaction and refund processing costs.

## Credit Purchases

Credit purchases are **non-refundable** unless required by law or approved by support for an exceptional case. We recommend starting with a smaller package if you are unsure about usage.

## Subscription Plan Changes

If you request a change from an annual subscription to a monthly plan:

- One month of subscription fees plus applicable processing fees may be deducted
- The remaining eligible balance may be refunded to your original payment method
- Approved refunds typically process within 5-7 business days

## Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally non-refundable:

- Credit purchases
- Subscription time that has already passed
- Processing fees and transaction charges
- Subscriptions beyond the 3-day refund window
- Subscriptions where more than 20 credits have been used

## Refund Process

To request a refund:

1. Contact support at [support@dreamomni.ai](mailto:support@dreamomni.ai)
2. Include your account email, order or transaction ID, and refund reason
3. Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours
4. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

## Special Circumstances

If you believe your case warrants special consideration outside the standard policy, contact support with detailed information.

## Policy Updates

DreamOmni may update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective when posted.
