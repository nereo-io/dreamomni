# Refund Policy

## Introduction

At **Seedance**, we strive to provide exceptional AI-powered video generation services. We understand that sometimes our service may not meet your expectations, and we've established this refund policy to ensure fair treatment for all our customers.

## Subscription Refunds

### Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a subscription refund, you must:

- Request the refund within **3 days** of your initial purchase
- Have used **20 credits or fewer** during the subscription period
- Submit your refund request through our official support channels

### Processing Fees

Please note that processing fees will be deducted from your refund amount. These fees cover the transaction costs associated with processing payments and refunds.

## Credit Purchases

**Important**: Credit purchases are **non-refundable**. We encourage all customers to:

- Carefully consider your credit needs before purchasing
- Start with smaller credit packages if you're unsure about your usage requirements
- Take advantage of our subscription plans which may offer better value for regular users

## Subscription Plan Changes

If you wish to change from an annual subscription to a monthly plan:

- You may request this change at any time
- One month's subscription fee plus applicable processing fees will be deducted
- The remaining balance will be refunded to your original payment method
- Refunds typically process within 5-7 business days

## Non-Refundable Items

The following items are explicitly non-refundable:

- Credit purchases (all credit packages)
- Subscription time that has already passed
- Processing fees and transaction charges
- Subscriptions beyond the 3-day refund window
- Subscriptions where more than 20 credits have been used

## Refund Process

To request a refund:

1. Contact our support team at [support@seedance.tv](mailto:support@seedance.tv)
2. Include the following information:
   - Your account email address
   - Order or transaction ID
   - Reason for requesting the refund
3. Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours
4. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

## Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional situations may arise. If you believe your case warrants special consideration outside our standard policy, please contact our support team with detailed information about your situation.

## Policy Updates

Seedance reserves the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. By continuing to use our services, you acknowledge and accept any updates to this policy.

## Customer Acknowledgment

By using Seedance's services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this refund policy. We recommend reviewing this policy before making any purchases.
