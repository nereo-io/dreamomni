# Privacy Policy for BaziAI

## Introduction

Welcome to BaziAI, your trusted AI-powered destiny reading platform. At BaziAI, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with accurate and personalized destiny readings:

1. **Birth Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Your birth date, time, and location, which are essential for generating accurate destiny readings.
   - **Purpose**: To perform personalized destiny analysis and provide accurate readings.

2. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Your name, email address, and account preferences.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide customer support, and deliver personalized services.

3. **Reading History**

   - **What We Collect**: Records of your readings, questions asked, and responses received.
   - **Purpose**: To provide consistent and contextual readings, and improve our service quality.

4. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data about the devices you use to access BaziAI, including device type and browser information.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our service for different devices and ensure smooth operation.

5. **Payment Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Payment method details and transaction records.
   - **Purpose**: To process payments for premium readings and manage subscriptions.

## Data Storage and Security

We understand the sensitive nature of your personal information, especially birth details. All data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers. We implement strict security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your information. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

- When required by law or to comply with legal processes

- With trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform, subject to strict confidentiality agreements
