# Privacy Policy for GeminiOmni

## Introduction

Welcome to GeminiOmni, an independent AI video generation platform. We take privacy seriously and use personal information only to operate, secure, improve, and support the service.

GeminiOmni.tv is not affiliated with Google, Gemini, or Google DeepMind.

## Information Collection and Use

We may collect the following data:

1. **Video Generation Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Prompts, uploaded inputs, generation settings, model choices, and generation results.
   - **Purpose**: To process requests, provide generation history, and support troubleshooting.

2. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Name, email address, authentication provider, account preferences, and support messages.
   - **Purpose**: To manage accounts, authentication, customer support, and service notifications.

3. **Usage and Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Browser, device, IP-derived region, page usage, and diagnostic logs.
   - **Purpose**: To secure the service, detect abuse, improve performance, and understand product usage.

4. **Payment Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Transaction records and subscription status. Payment card details are handled by the payment provider and are not stored directly by GeminiOmni.
   - **Purpose**: To process purchases, manage credits, and provide billing support.

## Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information and creative content. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

- With service providers who help operate hosting, database, authentication, analytics, payments, email, or AI generation
- When required by law or valid legal process
- To protect our rights, users, platform security, or the public
- With your consent

## Use of Generated Content

Prompts, uploaded inputs, and generated outputs are used to provide the requested service, maintain generation history, investigate support issues, and prevent abuse. We do not share your generated content with third parties for marketing without your consent.

Provider-specific processing rules may apply when a generation request is sent to an AI model provider.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
