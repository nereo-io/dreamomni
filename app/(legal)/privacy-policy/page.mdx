# Privacy Policy for Veo3AI

## Introduction

Welcome to Veo3AI, your trusted AI-powered video generation platform. At Veo3AI, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with high-quality video generation services:

1. **Video Generation Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Your video prompts, generation requests, and preferences for creating AI-generated videos.
   - **Purpose**: To process your video generation requests and provide personalized video creation services.

2. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Your name, email address, and account preferences.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide customer support, and deliver personalized services.

3. **Generation History**

   - **What We Collect**: Records of your video generations, prompts used, and settings applied.
   - **Purpose**: To provide consistent service quality, improve our AI models, and offer personalized recommendations.

4. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data about the devices you use to access Veo3AI, including device type and browser information.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our service for different devices and ensure smooth video generation and playback.

5. **Payment Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Payment method details and transaction records.
   - **Purpose**: To process payments for premium video generation services and manage subscriptions.

## Data Storage and Security

We understand the importance of protecting your creative content and personal information. All data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers. We implement strict security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your information. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

- When required by law or to comply with legal processes
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- With trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform, subject to strict confidentiality agreements

## Use of Video Content

Your video generation requests and created content are used solely for:

- Processing and generating your requested videos
- Improving our AI video generation algorithms
- Providing technical support and service optimization
  We never share your generated videos or prompts with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
