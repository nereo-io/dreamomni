# GeminiOmni - Gemini Omni Video Generator and Model Tracker

## Project Overview

GeminiOmni (geminiomni.tv) is an independent AI video platform focused on Gemini Omni news, model availability, and practical video generation workflows. The site tracks Gemini Omni rollout status while offering text-to-video and image-to-video creation through currently available providers.

GeminiOmni.tv is not affiliated with Google, Gemini, or Google DeepMind.

## Gemini Omni Status

Google announced Gemini Omni at Google I/O 2026 as a new multimodal video generation family. The first announced model is Gemini Omni Flash, designed to create or edit video from combinations of text, image, audio, and video inputs.

Native Gemini Omni generation is not enabled on GeminiOmni.tv until an integrated provider exposes a real Gemini Omni endpoint. Public KIE.ai docs and market pages checked during launch preparation did not list Gemini Omni support.

## Core Features

### Video Generation
- **Text-to-Video**: Generate videos from text prompts through supported models
- **Image-to-Video**: Animate images into dynamic video content
- **Model Availability Tracking**: Follow Gemini Omni provider status and rollout updates
- **Multiple Available Models**: Use supported video models while Gemini Omni integration is pending
- **Configurable Output**: Choose aspect ratio, duration, quality, and model settings where supported

### User Experience
- **Fast Landing Page**: Gemini Omni-focused product page for launch traffic
- **Video History**: Track, play, download, and manage generated videos
- **Authentication**: Google and email sign-in flows are planned for production launch
- **Multi-language Foundation**: Locale-aware routing for international landing pages

### Payment System
- Stripe support is planned. Paid plans and credit packages should only be promoted after production Stripe configuration is completed.

## Technical Architecture

### Frontend
- **Next.js 14** with App Router and Server Components
- **TypeScript** for type safety
- **Tailwind CSS** for responsive UI
- **shadcn/ui and Radix** for interface primitives

### Backend and Infrastructure
- **Supabase** for PostgreSQL and auth-related storage
- **Vercel** for production deployment
- **Cloudflare DNS** for geminiomni.tv
- **Provider Webhooks** for generation status updates

### AI Integration
- Existing generation flows support currently integrated providers.
- Gemini Omni should remain clearly marked as pending until a real provider endpoint is live.
